# Changelog

## [v1.0.0]

### Added
- **Customizable Password Options**:  
  Easily generate passwords with customizable options, including:  
  - Numbers  
  - Uppercase letters  
  - Lowercase letters  
  - Special characters  

- **Fixed High-Strength Passwords**:  
  Generate consistent and secure passwords by combining:  
  - A master password  
  - A unique salt  
  - A version number  

- **Enhanced Security**:  
  Utilizes cryptographic techniques to ensure passwords are highly secure and unique.

- **User-Friendly Design**:  
  Designed with an intuitive interface for quick and easy password customization and generation.
